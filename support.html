Yes! PicSwipe processes all photos locally on your device. We never upload your photos to our servers or collect any personal data. Your photos stay 100% private.

Yes! Deleted photos are moved to your device's "Recently Deleted" album where they remain for 30 days before permanent deletion. You can recover them anytime within this period.
