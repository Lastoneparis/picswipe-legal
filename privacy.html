Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 14, 2025

PicSwipe ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the PicSwipe mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Photo Access

PicSwipe requires access to your photo library to provide its core functionality of photo organization and cleanup. We access your photos only on your device and do not upload, store, or transmit your photos to any external servers.

1.2 Usage Information

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app, including:

  • Number of photos reviewed
  • App features used
  • Session duration
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Crash reports and performance data

1.3 Purchase Information

When you make in-app purchases, payment information is processed by Apple's App Store. We receive only transaction confirmation and subscription status information, never your payment details.

1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your actual photos or their content
  • Your name or personal identity
  • Your email address (unless you contact support)
  • Your location data beyond what's already in your photos' metadata
  • Your contacts or other personal information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide core functionality: Access your photos to enable organization and cleanup features
  • Improve the app: Analyze anonymous usage patterns to enhance features
  • Process subscriptions: Manage your Premium subscription status
  • Fix bugs: Identify and resolve technical issues
  • Provide support: Respond to your inquiries if you contact us

3. Data Storage and Processing

3.1 Local Processing

All photo processing happens locally on your device. Your photos never leave your device and are not uploaded to any cloud service or external server.

3.2 Subscription Data

Subscription information is stored securely and processed through Apple's App Store infrastructure.

3.3 Analytics

Anonymous usage statistics may be stored on secure servers for the purpose of app improvement. This data cannot be linked back to you personally.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4.1 Service Providers

We may share anonymous data with:

  • Apple App Store: For payment processing and subscription management
  • Analytics providers: For anonymous usage statistics (if applicable)
  • Crash reporting services: To identify and fix bugs

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Photo Access Control

You can revoke photo library access at any time through your device settings (Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos → PicSwipe).

5.2 Subscription Management

You can view, modify, or cancel your subscription at any time through your App Store account settings.

5.3 Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data from your device
  • Contact us at hugomoriceau@gmail.com to request deletion of any server-stored anonymous usage data

6. Children's Privacy

PicSwipe is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • All processing happens locally on your device
  • Subscription data is protected by Apple's security infrastructure
  • Anonymous usage data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We regularly review our security practices

8. International Data Transfers

Since all photo processing happens locally on your device, your photos are never transferred internationally. Any anonymous analytics data may be stored on servers located in various countries, including the United States and European Union.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Displaying an in-app notification for significant changes

Your continued use of PicSwipe after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Object: Object to processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent: At any time

12. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

  • Consent: You grant permission to access your photos
  • Contract: Processing is necessary to provide our services
  • Legitimate interests: Improving our app and preventing fraud
